angle accuracy: —

Stand at the center and tap Set Center. Walk out along your desired 0° direction and tap Set 0° Line. The needle then shows your angle off that line — keep it in the green zone to stay on bearing. The ± accuracy is computed from GPS accuracy and distance: a longer baseline (e.g. 100 m) makes the same GPS scatter span a much smaller angle, so walk further out for a sharper, more confident reading.
